How to play

  • Pick a word pack and the number of players (and imposters).
  • Pass the phone — everyone secretly peeks at their word.
  • Take turns giving a one-word clue, then discuss and vote out a suspect.
  • Civilians win by finding every imposter; imposters win by blending in.

Frequently asked questions

How do I unlock all the word packs?

  • Open a locked pack (or the Shop) and tap Unlock Everything. It's a one-time purchase that unlocks every current and future pack.

I bought the unlock but it's not showing / I reinstalled.

  • Go to Settings → Restore Purchases in the app. This re-grants your unlock on any device signed into the same Apple ID. Make sure you're signed in with the Apple ID you purchased with.

Do I need an internet connection?

  • No — the game is pass-and-play and works fully offline. An internet connection is only used to fetch new word packs and to process purchases.

How many players can play?

  • 4 to 11 players on a single phone.

How do I request a refund?

  • Purchases are handled by Apple. Request refunds at reportaproblem.apple.com.

Found a bug or have a suggestion?

  • We'd love to hear it — email coolcontent1992@gmail.com with your device model and iOS version.
