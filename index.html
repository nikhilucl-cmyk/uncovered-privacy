How it works
Three minutes to learn. All night to argue.
1

Pass & peek

Everyone secretly sees their word. Most players share the same one — but the imposter gets a different word, and Mr. White gets nothing at all.

2

Describe & deceive

Take turns dropping one-word clues about your secret word. Say too much and you expose yourself; too little and you look guilty.

3

Vote it out

Debate, accuse, and vote. Catch the imposter to win — or watch them bluff their way to victory.
