Privacy Policy

Uncovered

The Imposter Party Game · Last updated: 1 June 2026

The short version: Uncovered is a pass-and-play party game. No account is required, and we do not collect personal information that identifies you. The game works offline. Player names you type stay on your device and are never sent to us.

Information we handle

Stored only on your device

  • Player names entered for a game — kept in local storage on your device, never uploaded.
  • Settings & progress — your sound preference and pack-unlock status, stored locally (AsyncStorage).

Word pack content (network)

  • The app fetches word packs from our backend (Supabase). These requests are anonymous and contain no personal data. When offline, the app uses packs bundled with the app.

Purchases

  • In-app purchases (e.g. “Unlock Everything”) are processed by the Apple App Store or Google Play, depending on your device. We never receive or store your payment details — the store handles the transaction and shares only confirmation that a purchase succeeded. Purchase status is managed via RevenueCat to unlock content and to support “Restore Purchases.”

Advertising

  • We do not currently show ads. If we add optional rewarded ads in a future version, we will update this policy and disclose the ad provider before that version ships.

Children’s privacy

The app is intended for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us and we will delete it.

Data sharing

We do not sell your information. Data is shared only with the service providers needed to operate the app — Supabase (pack content), Apple and RevenueCat (purchase processing & entitlement) — strictly to provide the service.

Your choices

  • Gameplay and settings data stay on your device; clearing the app’s storage or uninstalling removes them.
  • You can manage purchases in your Apple ID or Google Play account settings.

Data retention

Locally-stored data persists until you clear it or uninstall. We do not maintain user accounts or server-side profiles.

Changes

We may update this policy; the “Last updated” date will change. Material changes will be noted in-app or on this page.
